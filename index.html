My Story Worlds

Personalized Stories, Endless Discoveries

Learning to read should be fun!

What if there was a way to avoid fighting with your children to get them reading?

We believe that when young children between 5 and 8 years old are forced to read material that is of no interest, or even just plain boring, to them, they tend to view reading as a chore. We offer instead to help you create something so attractive to them, that they will actually be excited to read.

My Story Worlds helps parents create stories, games and mini-documentaries completely tailored to their childs interest

Parents will now have access to tools to easily write stories, create games and build mini-documentaries. Add exciting images to your story, highlight difficult words, attach sounds to help with pronounciation, provide links to topics they love, create charades or guessing games, and so much more.
